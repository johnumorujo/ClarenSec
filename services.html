Explore Our Services

Penetration Testing

We simulate real world cyber attacks on your healthcare systems, infrastructure and applications to identify vulnerabilities before malicious actors exploit them. Our assessments are tailored for hospital networks, EMRs, and digital health apps.

Security Advisory & Compliance

Our cyber security experts guide you through NDPA, ISO 270011 and other cybersecurity frameworks. We help you create, review and implement policies, SOPs and technical safeguards to remain secure and compliant.

Red Team Assessments

Clarence deploys simulated threat actor tactics against your security posture, testing everything from physical access controls to phishing response. We go beyond the firewall to test your organization's ability to detect and respond to advanced threats.

Healthcare Cybersecurity Training

We offer on-site and virtual training programs designed for healthcare professionals, IT staff, and leadership. Topics include phishing awareness, secure data handling, and incident response readiness.

Incident Response & Recovery Planning

We help organizations build effective incident response and disaster recovery strategies before disaster strikes. From logging policies through attack response workflows to ensuring near-zero service downtime, we help organizations recover with speed and confidence.

Security Architecture Reviews

Our team evaluates your current IT environment, medical device integrations, and third-party dependencies to design resilient, secure systems from the ground up.

Our mission is to safeguard health data; one of the most sensitive forms of personal information, ensuring it remains secure and confidential at all times.
