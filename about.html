Who we Are!

Clarence Sec Ltd is a cybersecurity firm dedicated to protecting the most sensitive medical information. Founded by Dr. John Umoru, a medical doctor turned cybersecurity expert, the company was born from his unique blend of healthcare and technical cybersecurity experience. Dr. Umoru has over four years of experience securing digital systems and responsibly disclosing critical vulnerabilities to countless companies across the globe.
He saw first-hand that many, if not all, Nigerian hospitals lacked robust cybersecurity, leaving patient records exposed. Rooted in his Hippocratic commitment to patient confidentiality, he launched Clarence Sec to bridge this gap and ensure that patient data is never compromised

Our Mission

Clarence Sec was established to meet this critical need. Our mission is simple but vital: to ensure that health data – one of the most sensitive categories of personal information – is always protected. We apply Dr. Umoru’s medical insight and cybersecurity expertise to safeguard clinics, hospitals, and health networks. By providing world-class vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, red team assessments, and security consulting, we build strong defenses around patient information

Our Core Commitments.

  • Patient Privacy First: We honor the physician’s duty of confidentiality, putting patient data protection above all.
  • Technical Excellence: We leverage proven global security practices and expert vulnerability research to fortify healthcare IT systems
  • Focused on Healthcare: We specifically address the unique challenges of medical institutions, filling the gap left by under-resourced security in Nigeria’s health sector.
  • Protecting Sensitive Data: We recognize that health information is legally and ethically highly sensitive, and we strive to keep it secure at all times.

Clarence Sec stands as a guardian of patient information, ensuring that hospitals can focus on healing while we focus on defending. Our unwavering goal is to uphold patients’ trust by keeping their most private health data safe from digital threats
