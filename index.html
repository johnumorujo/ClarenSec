Why Healthcare Security Matters

Hospitals and clinics are going digital, but security often gets left behind. Around the world, healthcare is one of the easiest targets for cyber criminals, and Nigeria is no exception. Many Hospital facilities are just beginning to use electronic medical records and telemedicine platforms, often without proper safeguards in place.

The Risk

Cyber criminals are increasingly targeting healthcare. In Nigeria, patient records can be stolen and used for blackmail, fraud, or identity theft.
A single ransomware attack can lock you out of your hospital systems.

The Impact

Trust is lost.
Surgeries are delayed.
Patient care is disrupted.
Recovery is expensive, and in healthcare, the cost of downtime can be measured in lives lost.

The Solution

ClarenSec protects healthcare with practical cybersecurity. From penetration tests and red team exercises to compliance audits and staff training. Our tailored approach ensures security improves clinical care without slowing it down.
